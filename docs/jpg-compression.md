# JPG Compression

JPG, also called JPEG, is one of the most common image formats for photos, camera images, portraits, product photos and website pictures.

JPG compression reduces file size by lowering some visual detail. A good quality setting can make the image much smaller while keeping it clear enough for websites, forms, email and sharing.

## When to Use JPG

Use JPG for:

- Camera photos
- Product images
- Blog images
- Website banners
- Profile photos
- Job application photos
- Online form photos

## Best JPG Compression Settings

A good starting point is:

- Quality: 75% to 85%
- Max width: 1200px to 1800px
- Format: JPG/JPEG

For online forms, you may need a smaller target size such as 50KB, 100KB or 200KB.
